# Adding More Parts

Once the base prototype works, expand carefully.

## Expansion ideas

- Rotary encoder for quick navigation
- OLED display for status and menus
- RGB indicators for feedback
- Extra switches for dedicated actions

## Before adding parts

1. Confirm your current power budget.
2. Reserve GPIO pins in your wiring plan.
3. Verify physical clearances in your enclosure.

## Practical checklist

- Label your wires and headers
- Test each part in isolation first
- Merge one hardware change at a time
- Document every pin mapping in your repo
